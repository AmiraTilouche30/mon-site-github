Best main Characters

Best characters

Gojo Saturu

Satoru Gojo is a highly skilled and charismatic sorcerer in Jujutsu Kaisen. He's a mentor to the main protagonist, Yuji Itadori, and is known for his playful demeanor, incredible combat abilities, and the use of the Limitless Cursed Technique, which makes him one of the most powerful characters in the series.

Hak

Hak Son is the loyal bodyguard of Princess Yona. He's skilled in combat and has a protective nature towards Yona, whom he's known since childhood. Hak is strong, witty, and fiercely devoted to Yona's safety, even as they embark on a journey to reclaim her kingdom.

Eren Yeager

is the main protagonist of Attack on Titan. Initially introduced as a determined and somewhat hot-headed young boy living within the walls of Paradis Island, Eren witnesses the sudden breach of the colossal titan that leads to the destruction of his hometown and the loss of his mother. Fueled by a deep desire for freedom and fueled by a vengeful spirit against the Titans, Eren vows to eradicate them.

Kou

Kou Mabuchi is a key character in Blue Spring Ride. Initially distant and reserved, he's a former classmate of the protagonist, Futaba Yoshioka. Kou harbors a complex past and undergoes personal growth throughout the series, revealing a caring and sensitive side despite his initial standoffish demeanor.

yamada

Yamada is the main protagonist of LV999 no Murabito (The Villager of Level 999). Born with the lowest level in a world governed by levels, he possesses an ability that allows him to gain all skills and powers he observes. He sets out on a journey to become the strongest in a world ruled by the concept of levels.

Yuu

Yuu Otosaka is the main character in Charlotte. Initially using his ability to possess others for personal gain, he gets recruited by Nao Tomori into Hoshinoumi Academy to prevent others from misusing their powers. Yuu undergoes significant character development, growing from a selfish person to someone who cares deeply about his friends and their welfare.

Kakashi

Kakashi Hatake is a highly skilled ninja known as "Copy Ninja Kakashi." He's a former ANBU captain and the mentor of Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke Uchiha, and Sakura Haruno. Kakashi is calm, enigmatic, and possesses the Sharingan, which allows him to copy techniques. He's famous for his "Makeout Paradise" book and his adherence to the rules.

Inuyasha

Inuyasha is a half-demon, half-human character in the anime series. He seeks the Shikon Jewel to become a full demon but later allies with Kagome Higurashi to collect the scattered shards of the jewel. Inuyasha is headstrong, courageous, and possesses enhanced strength, speed, and the ability to wield the Tessaiga sword.

Denji

Denji is the main character in Chainsaw Man, living a rough life by hunting devils to pay off his deceased father's debt. After a series of events, he merges with his pet devil, Pochita, becoming a devil-human hybrid with chainsaw-like powers. Denji is initially naive but resilient, driven by simple desires like food and a normal life.
